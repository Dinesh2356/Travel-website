Our story

From a frustrating layover to a full-time obsession

Wanderly began in 2019 when our founders, stuck on a delayed layover, spent three hours trying to rebook a hotel through a clunky app that kept timing out. They figured there had to be a better way — and built it.

What started as a weekend side-project comparing hotel prices across three cities has grown into a platform used by travelers across India and beyond, built by a small team that still personally tests every feature before it ships.

What drives us

Mission & vision

Our Mission

To make booking travel so simple and transparent that planning a trip feels as good as taking one — no hidden fees, no confusing steps, no guesswork.

Our Vision

A world where anyone, anywhere, can discover and book a place to stay in under a minute — with the confidence that comes from real reviews and real prices.
